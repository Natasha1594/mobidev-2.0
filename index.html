About Us

MOBIDEV

Mobidev Kenya LTD started with a vision to set the standards in Enterprise grade software developement in Sub Saharan Africa. Since inception, we have engaged several companies, organizations and individuals to take advantage of the emerging technologies in the mobile field. Our technical strength is founded on hands on experience through the solutions we have deployed in a number of countries: Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Nigeria and Somalia. We have handled major projects for clients including UAP Insurance (Kenya), The British Council (Kenya and Nigeria), Health Consortium Somalia (Kenya and Somalia), just to mention a few. We help clients to not only implement specifications but also think through each solution and its suitability in addressing the problem adequately and cost effectively.

We make use of mobile and web technologies to extend and build value for our clients in industries such as insurance, media companies, E-learning and M-learning companies, NGOs, E-Commerce companies and other various SMEs. We value building next-generation, native and cross-platform mobile apps running on Android, iOS, Windows Phone and Blackberry devices. Apps we build are not only great to use but also create the highest value possible for our clients. We don’t stop there — our apps have future integrations in mind and provide robust APIs that stand the test of time. We specialize in building great Web apps. Ours apps are optimized and tested to work seamlessly with different devices: phones, tablets, PCs and TVs too! We not only develop applications for our clients but we also work closely with them throughout the process to bring out the best of their concepts through our competence, processes and strong component frameworks.
